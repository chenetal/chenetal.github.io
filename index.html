Dr. Xiaowei Chen

Postdoctoral Fellow · Human-Centered Security and Privacy Group
Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy, Bochum, Germany

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of human-computer interaction, security & privacy, and behavioral science. My research designs, deploys, and evaluates security and privacy interventions in the field. Before joining MPI-SP, I worked as a doctoral researcher at the University of Luxembourg and completed my PhD in Psychology (Human-Computer Interaction) in September 2025.

I currently lead the SiCV project ("Supporting Infrastructure for Cybercrime Victims"), aiming to thoroughly examine the recovery process of cybercrime victims in EU states and to design social and technical supporting infrastructures to aid their recovery. I work with key stakeholders from service platforms, law enforcement, and financial institutions to address the challenges posed by emerging cybercrimes.
